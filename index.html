Welcome to macrow by Viraj!
Simplified macroeconomic simulator for IB macroeconomic policy on the Keynesian AD/AS diagram.
Macrow is a tool created to empower students with a deep understanding of Macro(w)economics.

🧪 Prototype goal

Built first as a classroom sandbox to test Keynesian AD-AS intuition before formal exam write-ups.

📉 What it demonstrates

How demand, costs, and productivity shift curves, move equilibrium, and change output and price outcomes.

🧭 Current role

Still a prototype-style learning tool: fast to explore, clear for teaching, and easy to share with scenarios.
