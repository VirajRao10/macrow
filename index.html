Welcome to macrow by Viraj!
Simplified Macro(w)economic simulator for IB macroeconomic policy on the Keynesian AD/AS diagram. Currently a prototype!
macrow is designed to show a simplified version of macroeconomic events on the IB Keynesian AD–AS diagram. Its purpose is to help IB students grasp these concepts so they can be applied effectively in exams.
Add macrow to your Home Screen (further optimisation coming soon)
  1. iPhone (Safari): Share → Add to Home Screen.
  2. Android (Chrome): Menu → Install app / Add to Home screen.
  3. Open macrow from your Home Screen for a full-screen app-like layout.
  4. Or access on Desktop by copying macrow.virajrao.com
